## Detailed description

```
Ever seen the memes claiming every AI prompt consumes a bottle of water? waterusedbyourAi turns that curiosity into an honest, on-screen number.

When you chat on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity, waterusedbyourAi shows a small dashboard card below each finished reply, estimating the cumulative water, energy, and CO2 footprint for the conversation.

The real numbers are small. A single text prompt consumes a fraction of a milliliter, not the entire bottles suggested by viral memes. Credible figures put a typical text query around a quarter of a milliliter. waterusedbyourAi transparently calculates and displays this footprint.

- Honest by default: real, order-of-magnitude estimates, clearly labelled.
- Comprehensive metrics: reports water (mL), energy (Wh), and carbon (mg CO2e) values.
- Clean updates: waits for the reply to finish to avoid interface flickering.
- Privacy first: runs entirely locally in your browser with no external network requests.

How it works:
1. Translates conversation text into tokens.
2. Estimates energy consumption (Watt-hours) based on the active model profile.
3. Computes the corresponding water and carbon overhead using regional power grid mixes and datacenter cooling estimates.

All coefficients and source code are open-source and documented in the repository. Not affiliated with Google, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Perplexity.
```
