# Privacy Policy — waterusedbyourAi

Last updated: 2026-07-04

waterusedbyourAi collects no user data. It runs entirely inside your browser.

## What data the extension accesses

- The extension reads the visible conversation text (your messages and the AI's replies) on active chat tabs to estimate token counts. This parsing happens locally within the page to compute the inline footprint meter.

## What the extension does with this data

- It converts the token metrics into estimates of water, energy, and carbon footprint, then renders the results in an inline card.

## What the extension does NOT do

- No servers: waterusedbyourAi makes no network requests. Your conversation text never leaves your device.
- No content storage: It does not save, log, or transmit your prompts or replies.
- No tracking: No telemetry, analytics, advertising trackers, cookies, or fingerprinting are employed.
- No sharing: Since no data is collected, none is sold, shared, or compiled.

## Permissions

waterusedbyourAi requests permissions to run only on matching AI chat domains. It requests no other permissions (no tabs, browser history, clipboard, or background process access).

For questions or issues, please open an issue in the project's GitHub repository.
